Why stake my ADA?

Staking ADA is a secure way to earn interest on your hard earned coins and you can do that directly from within your own Yoroi or Daedalus wallet. The best part is that ADA never leaves your wallet and you retain full control over any and all funds that you deligate to a pool.

Earnings are automatically paid out, by the Cardano protocol, to your wallet every five days.

Please make sure only to use official versions of any wallet you choose.
